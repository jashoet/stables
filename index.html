About The Stables

The Stables is more than just a church - we're a community hub dedicated to serving the people of Onkaparinga with compassion and practical support.

Our mission is to provide help, healing, and hope through our various programs including the Op Shop, food assistance, and community support initiatives.

We also support school chaplaincy and community projects in the Pacific, extending our reach beyond our local community.
